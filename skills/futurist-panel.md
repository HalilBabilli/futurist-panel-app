# Futurist Panel - Multi-Perspective Analysis Orchestrator

## Purpose

Orchestrate a structured analysis where multiple specialist perspectives examine a topic, followed by an integrated synthesis from the Futurist Prime.

## How to Use

When you want comprehensive multi-lens analysis, invoke this skill with:

```
/futurist-panel [TOPIC]
```

Or simply say: "Run a futurist panel on [TOPIC]"

## Quick Panel Variations

### Deep Dive Panel (Longer)
Add more detail to each analyst section. Use when topic is complex and you have time.

### Quick Panel (Shorter)
Each analyst gives 1 paragraph. Synthesis is bullet points only. Use for rapid assessment.

### Focused Panel (Subset)
Only use 2-3 most relevant analysts + Futurist Prime. Use when topic is narrow.

### Debate Panel
Have analysts explicitly disagree and argue. Futurist Prime mediates. Use for controversial topics.

## Example Invocations

- "Run a futurist panel on quantum computing's impact on cybersecurity"
- "Quick panel: Will remote work persist?"
- "Deep dive panel on the future of higher education"
- "Debate panel: Is UBI inevitable due to AI automation?"
- "Focused panel (tech + geopolitical + synthesis) on semiconductor supply chains"

## Panel Etiquette

Each analyst should:
- Stay in their lane (their specialty)
- Acknowledge uncertainty appropriately
- Be concise but substantive
- Reference other analysts' points when relevant
- Avoid repeating what others said

Futurist Prime should:
- Genuinely synthesize, not just summarize
- Identify non-obvious connections
- Call out where analysts' views conflict
- Provide actionable conclusions
- Maintain intellectual humility
